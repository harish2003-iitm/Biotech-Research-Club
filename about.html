We are a bunch of students who promote, discuss and encourage research in bio and allied fields! We organise talks, quizzes and have a monthly themed newsletter highlighting some upcoming conferences/talks and articles by students!/p>

ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

The batch of 2017 students did their mandatory core internship in the summer of 2019.To formalise the discussion on what each of them had learnt over the summer, we kickstarted the Biotech Research Club (BRC).Along with work presentations, we also found volunteers who presented research articles.

Our Motive

Our motive is to create a platform for students to share their experiences and learn from each other. We also aim to create a network of students and alumni who can help each other out in the future.

We have many students who have experience of research in various topics or sub-branches of biotechnology. BRC gives an opportunity to showcase these so that future batches will have reference to move on or what exactly they have to do.Fundaes of various research opportunities including international offers.Exposure of Biotech industry by keeping sessions/talksOur sessions are open to all students and we encourage everyone to attend them.
